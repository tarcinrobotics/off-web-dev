Course Overview

In this course, you'll embark on a journey that begins with the fundamentals of cloud computing, including the various service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) and deployment models (public, private, hybrid). You'll learn to harness the potential of cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, gaining hands-on experience with cloud services such as virtual machines, containers, storage, and serverless computing. As you progress, you'll explore best practices for cloud security, data management, and cost optimization, ensuring you can confidently architect and manage cloud solutions. Whether you're an aspiring cloud engineer, developer, or business professional, this course offers a pathway to navigate the cloud landscape, unlock efficiency, and drive innovation in the digital age."
